Overview

Max Extrusions is the official corporate website for a leading manufacturer in the aluminium extrusion industry. The platform serves as both a digital brand presence and a product discovery tool for B2B buyers, contractors, architects, and fabricators searching for certified aluminium extrusion profiles and custom manufacturing services.

The website positions Max Extrusions as a credible, modern manufacturer in a traditionally offline industry, giving the company a strong digital foundation for B2B lead generation and customer acquisition.

Problem It Solves

Industrial manufacturers in India often have outdated, slow, non-mobile-friendly websites that fail to convert technical buyers into inquiries. Max Extrusions needed a modern digital presence that could represent their manufacturing capabilities clearly, showcase their full product range with technical specifications, and generate qualified leads from contractors and bulk buyers.

The project required translating complex industrial domain knowledge into a clean, accessible digital experience — balancing technical detail with visual clarity to serve both engineers reviewing specifications and procurement managers comparing suppliers.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive product catalog with technical specification sheets and profile dimensions
  • Industry-specific landing sections for construction, automotive, and interior design segments
  • Inquiry form with product selection, quantity estimation, and custom requirement fields
  • Company profile showcasing certifications, manufacturing capacity, and quality standards
  • Mobile-first responsive design optimized for contractor on-site usage on phones
  • SEO-optimized content targeting industrial and B2B manufacturing search queries

Tech Stack

Built with Next.js for static site generation, ensuring fast page loads and full search engine crawlability — critical for B2B discovery through Google. Framer Motion adds professional micro-animations and scroll-triggered reveals that enhance the perceived quality of the brand without compromising load performance. Tailwind CSS enables precise control over the industrial aesthetic with a custom component design system. Hosted on Vercel with CDN distribution for consistent performance across all geographies.

Outcome

The Max Extrusions website is a live, production platform serving real B2B customers and representing an active manufacturing business online. The project demonstrates the ability to translate complex manufacturing domain requirements into a clean, effective digital platform — combining strong frontend engineering with an understanding of B2B customer psychology and industrial content strategy.
